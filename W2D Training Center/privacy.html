Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 16, 2026

01

Data collection

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal user data.

W2D Training Center is designed to function without requiring the user to provide personal information for normal use of the app.

02

Use of information

Since no personal user data is collected, no personal information is used for profiling, advertising, remarketing, resale, or behavioral tracking.

03

Data sharing

The Service Provider does not sell, rent, exchange, or disclose personal user data to third parties because such data is not collected by the app in the first place.

04

Children’s privacy

The application is not intended to collect personal data from children or from any other category of users. If a parent or guardian believes that information has been provided in error, they may contact the Service Provider for clarification.

05

Policy updates

Please note that this Privacy Policy may be revised occasionally to reflect any necessary updates. When such changes are made, the Service Provider will post the updated Privacy Policy on this page.

Users are encouraged to review this page periodically. Continued use of the application after updates means acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact
