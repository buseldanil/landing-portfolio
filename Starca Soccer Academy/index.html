Football Formations

A formation describes how a team positions its players. The best formation is the one that fits your players and exposes the opponent’s weaknesses.

4-4-2

ST ST LM CM CM RM LB CB CB RB GK

Balanced shape with good defensive structure.

4-3-3

LW ST RW CM CM CM LB CB CB RB GK

Aggressive pressing and wide attacking lanes.

Rules That Matter

Understand offside, cards, free kicks, penalties and throw-ins without getting lost in long explanations.

Yellow CardCaution for unsporting behavior, dissent or repeated fouls.
Red CardImmediate dismissal for serious foul play or DOGSO.
Penalty KickDirect shot for fouls inside the penalty area.
Indirect Free KickMust touch another player before a goal can be scored.

Tactical Thinking

Pressing styles and attack ideas help you read the rhythm of a match instead of only watching the ball.

HIGH PRESS

Win the ball back immediately after losing it. High-risk, high-reward.

MID BLOCK

Defend in your half but press when the opponent enters midfield.

LOW BLOCK

Compact defending with everyone behind the ball.
