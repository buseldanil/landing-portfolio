Privacy Policy

Jogos Santa Casa

The following Privacy Policy applies to the Jogos Santa Casa application.

1. Introduction

PLIUR organization has developed the Jogos Santa Casa app as a free service. The app does not collect, store, or share any personal user data.

2. Personal Data Collection and Retention

To provide a personalized experience within the application, we collect certain personal information, specifically your name.

3. Purpose of Collection

Purpose of Collection: This information is used exclusively for user identification and essential app functionality.

4. No Third-Party Sharing

No Third-Party Sharing: We strictly do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third-party advertisers, data brokers, or any external organizations.

5. Data Security and Encryption

Data Security and Encryption: All personal information is transmitted and stored using industry-standard encryption protocols (SSL/TLS) to ensure it remains protected from unauthorized access.

6. Retention and Deletion Policy

Retention and Deletion Policy: We adhere to a strict data minimization policy. Your name is automatically permanently deleted from our systems after 90 days of account inactivity.

7. User Control

User Control: You maintain full control over your data and may request manual deletion of your information at any time through the application settings.

8. Policy Updates

Please note that this Privacy Policy may be revised occasionally to reflect any necessary updates. When such changes are made, the Service Provider will post the updated Privacy Policy on this page. We encourage users to review this policy regularly, as continued use of the app implies acceptance of any modifications.

9. Contact

If you have any concerns regarding privacy while using the application or if you need further clarification on our practices, feel free to reach out to the Service Provider at devops@pliur.click.

10. Effective Date

This Privacy Policy is effective as of April 23, 2026.
